About Twenty One AI

Founded in 2019 by a team of AI researchers and enterprise automation experts, Twenty One AI emerged from a simple vision: to democratize artificial intelligence and make enterprise-grade automation accessible to businesses of all sizes. What started as a boutique consulting firm has evolved into a comprehensive automation agency, serving Fortune 500 companies and innovative startups alike. Our founding team recognized a critical gap in the market—while AI technology was advancing rapidly, most organizations lacked the expertise to implement it effectively. We set out to bridge that gap by combining cutting-edge technology with deep industry knowledge and proven implementation methodologies.

At Twenty One AI, our mission is to empower organizations to achieve operational excellence through intelligent automation. We believe that AI should augment human capability, not replace it, creating a synergy that drives unprecedented business growth. Our core values—innovation, integrity, and customer success—guide every decision we make. We're committed to staying at the forefront of AI and automation technology, continuously learning and evolving our solutions to meet the changing needs of our clients. With a team of 150+ professionals spanning data science, software engineering, process optimization, and change management, we deliver not just technology, but transformational business outcomes. Our track record speaks for itself: 500+ successful implementations, 98% client satisfaction rate, and over $2 billion in combined cost savings delivered to our clients.
